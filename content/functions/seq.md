`seq` creates a sequence of integers. It's named and used in the model of [GNU's seq][gnuseq].

```
3 → 1, 2, 3
1 2 4 → 1, 3
-3 → -1, -2, -3
1 4 → 1, 2, 3, 4
1 -2 → 1, 0, -1, -2
```

[gnuseq]: http://www.gnu.org/software/coreutils/manual/html_node/seq-invocation.html#seq-invocation
